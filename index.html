At Artfinder we love open source, from the existing projects we rely on, to code that we write that we like to share with the world.

Artfinder is your online guide to the world of art. From both famous and less well-known artworks, to the galleries you can see them in, we help you keep track of the art you know you like, and to find work you will love.

What we've released

  • django-render-as

    A templatetag to make it easy to render objects as modules with variations as required

  • jQuery equal height children

    A jQuery plugin to make elements have equal height.

  • searchtest

    Make it easy to track the effect of changes to search ranking or your position for searches on a public search engine.

  • awslib

    A small library containing various utilities for dealing with S3 & EC2 that we find handy.

  • django-jcache

    A cross between Django's caching architecture and the grace period rebuild feature in varnish. Depends on celery to asynchronously (re)generate cache entries.

  • django-moreviews

    Some class-based views that we found ourselves writing over and over.
